Community guidelines

A few ground rules

To maintain the trust and quality of this community, commercial members are expected to operate within the following guidelines.

  1. Content must be relevant to clinical practice, mental health, or AI tools used by clinicians
  2. No misleading claims about product capabilities, especially around clinical efficacy or AI accuracy
  3. Promotional content should be clearly identified as commercial — no disguised advertising
  4. Engagement should be genuine. Respond to questions and comments respectfully and honestly
  5. Privacy and confidentiality standards relevant to the clinical context must be maintained
  6. David reserves the right to remove content or membership that does not align with community values
