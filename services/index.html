About David Lopis

Australian registered psychologist, AI consultant, and board-approved supervisor with extensive experience in speaking engagements and thought leadership in the intersection of artificial intelligence and mental health care. Since early 2023, David has been at the forefront of AI integration in clinical practice, helping clinicians navigate the evolving landscape of responsible AI adoption in therapeutic settings.

As Director of Psychology Squared, David has built a thriving community of over 3,500 clinicians learning to use AI in therapy. His expertise spans training with major organizations including the Australian Psychological Society (APS), Australian Association of Psychologists Inc. (AAPi), and Australian Clinical Psychology Association (ACPA).
