Ethics, Compliance, and Legal

How do you address ethics?
We align with the Australian AI Ethics principles and professional competencies, emphasising transparency, accountability, privacy, and safety. See AI Ethical Standards.
What is the AI Compliance Pack?
A ready‑to‑use set of templates and guidance drafted by PGC Legal Pty Ltd: AI Policy, Privacy Policy, Consent Form for AI use, Implementation Checklist, and transcript inclusion guidance. Instant access after purchase. See AI Compliance Pack.
Who is the AI Compliance Pack for?
Psychologists using or planning to use AI. Individual licence is $199 (inc GST); practice bundles available on request.
Is the Compliance Pack legal advice? What are the limitations?
The content is informational, not legal advice, and comes with limitations of liability. Using it does not create a solicitor‑client relationship. See Important Legal Information.
