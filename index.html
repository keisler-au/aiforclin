About David Lopis

Australian registered psychologist, AI consultant, and board-approved supervisor with extensive experience in speaking engagements and thought leadership in artificial intelligence for clinicians especially focused on psychology and mental health care. Since early 2023, David has been pioneering discussions around AI use in clinical practice, helping clinicians navigate responsible AI adoption in therapeutic settings.

As Director of Psychology Squared, David has built a thriving community of over 3,500 clinicians learning to use AI in therapy on Facebook. He has presented for the Australian Psychological Society (APS), Australian Association of Psychologists Inc. (AAPi), and Australian Clinical Psychology Association (ACPA).
